Why Choose Prestige Academy?

Founded in 1995, Prestige Academy has established itself as a leader in quality education. Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education sets us apart.

  • 98% university placement rate
  • Certified and experienced teachers
  • Modern STEM and creative arts programs
  • Safe and nurturing environment

Our Programs

Comprehensive educational offerings for all ages

Preschool

A nurturing environment for our youngest learners ages 3-5, focusing on early literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

Elementary

Grades 1-5 with a strong foundation in core subjects, character development, and exploratory learning.

Middle School

Grades 6-8 with specialized subject teachers, leadership opportunities, and extracurricular activities.

High School

Grades 9-12 with college preparatory curriculum, AP courses, and career guidance.
