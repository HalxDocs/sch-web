Academic Excellence

Nigerian Curriculum with Global Standards - Preparing Students for WAEC, NECO, and Beyond

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive education from crèche to senior secondary

Early Years (Crèche - Kindergarten)

Our foundation program nurtures young minds through play-based learning and early literacy development, following the Nigerian Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) standards.

  • Montessori-inspired learning environment
  • Phonics and early numeracy program
  • Cultural appreciation (Nigerian languages & traditions)

Primary School (Basic 1-6)

Our primary program combines the Nigerian Universal Basic Education (UBE) curriculum with innovative teaching methods to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Core subjects: English, Mathematics, Basic Science & Technology
  • Cultural & Creative Arts with Nigerian heritage focus
  • Pre-vocational studies and ICT integration

Secondary School (JSS 1 - SSS 3)

Our secondary program prepares students for WAEC, NECO, and JAMB examinations while offering specialized tracks in sciences, arts, and business studies.

  • Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3): Broad-based curriculum
  • Senior Secondary (SSS 1-3): Science, Arts & Commercial streams
  • University preparation and career guidance

Curriculum Highlights

Blending Nigerian educational requirements with 21st century skills

Nigerian Curriculum Foundation

We adhere strictly to the Nigerian Educational Research and Development Council (NERDC) curriculum while enhancing it with our innovative teaching methodologies:

  • WAEC/NECO Preparation

    Comprehensive preparation for national examinations with 98% distinction rate in core subjects

  • Nigerian Languages

    Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa language options with cultural immersion programs

  • Civic Education

    Emphasizing Nigerian history, civic responsibility, and national unity

Global Perspectives

While rooted in Nigerian values, we prepare students for global competitiveness through:

  • STEM Education

    Advanced science, technology, engineering, and mathematics programs with robotics and coding

  • Cambridge Checkpoint

    Optional international curriculum for students seeking global qualifications

  • Exchange Programs

    Partnerships with schools in UK, US, and Canada for cultural exchange

Special Academic Programs

Enriching our curriculum with unique learning opportunities

JAMB Excellence Program

Comprehensive UTME preparation with mock exams and personalized coaching

Literary & Debate Society
