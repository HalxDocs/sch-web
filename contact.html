Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Prestige Academy

Admission requirements vary by grade level. Generally, we require completed application forms, previous school records, entrance examination results, and an interview. For specific requirements by grade, please visit our Admissions page.

Our tuition fees vary by grade level and include most educational materials. We offer flexible payment plans and sibling discounts. For a detailed fee structure, please contact our admissions office at admissions@prestigeacademy.edu.ng or call +234 812 345 6789.

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, swimming), arts (music, drama, visual arts), academic clubs (debate, science club), and community service programs. Activities vary by grade level and season. Visit our Academics page for more details.

Regular school hours are from 7:30 AM to 2:30 PM for elementary students and 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM for secondary students. We also offer after-school programs until 5:30 PM. Early drop-off is available from 6:30 AM for working parents.

Yes, we offer safe and reliable transportation services covering most areas in Lagos. Our buses are equipped with seat belts and are monitored by trained staff. Routes and fees vary depending on your location. Contact our transportation office for route availability and pricing.
