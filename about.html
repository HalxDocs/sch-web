About Prestige Academy

Our history, mission, and commitment to excellence in education

Our History

Prestige Academy was founded in 1995 with a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with moral instruction. What began as a small school with just 50 students has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in Nigeria.

Our founder, Chief Adebayo Ogunlesi, envisioned a school that would nurture future leaders through a balanced approach to education. Over the years, we've maintained this vision while adapting to the changing educational landscape.

Today, Prestige Academy boasts state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated staff, and a tradition of excellence that continues to grow with each graduating class.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops students intellectually, morally, and socially, preparing them to excel in their future endeavors while contributing positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that produces well-rounded individuals who are academically competent, morally upright, and capable of leadership in a global society.

Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all we do.

Integrity

We uphold honesty and strong moral principles.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and progressive thinking.

Community

We foster a supportive and inclusive environment.

Principal's Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-wishers,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Prestige Academy, a citadel of learning where we nurture young minds to become future leaders. Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education remains unwavering.

At Prestige, we believe every child is unique and possesses untapped potential. Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to provide an environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and moral uprightness.

As we continue our journey of excellence, we invite you to partner with us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Together, we can build a brighter future for our children and our nation.

Warm regards,

Dr. Adeola Johnson
Principal, Prestige Academy
