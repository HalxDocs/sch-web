Modern Facilities

Admission Process

Our straightforward 4-step admission procedure

1

Inquiry & Application

Submit an inquiry form online or visit our admission office. Complete the application form with all required documents.

  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Previous school report cards (for transfers)
  • 4 recent passport photographs
2

Entrance Assessment

All applicants will take an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness.

  • Primary: Basic literacy and numeracy test
  • Secondary: Subject-based assessment
  • Interview for senior secondary applicants
3

Admission Decision

Our admission committee reviews all applications and assessment results.

  • Notification within 7 working days
  • Acceptance letter and fee structure
  • Scholarship opportunities for exceptional candidates
4

Registration & Resumption

Complete the registration process and prepare for resumption.

  • Payment of required fees
  • Medical examination
  • Purchase of uniforms and textbooks
