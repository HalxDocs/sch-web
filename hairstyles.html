Grooming Guidelines

General Rules

  • All hairstyles must be neat and well-groomed at all times
  • Hair should be clean and free from excessive styling products
  • No extreme haircuts or unnatural colors are permitted
  • Hair should not cover the eyes or face

For Female Students

  • Braids should not extend beyond shoulder length
  • Weaves and extensions must be of natural colors (black, brown)
  • No elaborate designs or patterns shaved into the hair
  • Hair accessories should be simple and school-appropriate

For Male Students

  • Hair must be cut low (not exceeding 1 inch in length)
  • No afros, dreadlocks, or unkempt hairstyles
  • No designs, lines, or patterns cut into the hair
  • Facial hair must be clean-shaven at all times
